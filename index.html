Hisab Privacy Policy

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Hisab (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Mustafa Adam. The Application is a finance app where the grocery shops manage their customers accounts. Also, the users will be able to keep up to date with their accounts and check how their monthly hisab (bill) is calculated.
